About The Author

Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs writes faith centered books focused on healing, redemption, identity, and the presence of Jesus in places where pain often speaks the loudest.

The Sword She Never Asked For explores Mary’s journey through promise, suffering, silence, and peace while revealing how obedience continues even when understanding feels distant.

"Peace does not come from ease. Peace comes from alignment."

Stories throughout Bradley’s work invite readers toward hope while reminding hearts that Jesus remains present through every season.
